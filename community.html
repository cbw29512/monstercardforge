How an idea becomes part of DM Forge

1. DiscussShare the problem, use case, examples, and alternatives.
2. ValidateCommunity feedback and maintainer review clarify value, scope, privacy, and rules impact.
3. TrackAccepted work becomes a structured issue with tests, documentation, and release criteria.
4. ReleaseThe change ships only after the relevant design, rules, privacy, accessibility, and regression checks pass.

Community standards

Be useful, specific, respectful, and honest. Critique the tool or idea—not the person. Do not post private campaign secrets, personal information, pirated books, or proprietary D&D text that is not licensed for redistribution. Rules claims should link to an official source. Moderators may edit, lock, move, or remove content that does not follow the project’s Code of Conduct.
