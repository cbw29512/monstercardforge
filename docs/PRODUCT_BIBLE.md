# Product Bible

## Mission
Build the best physical and digital monster reference system for tabletop RPG Game Masters.

## Core Product Lines

- 5E 2014 SRD cards
- 5E 2024 SRD cards
- Homebrew cards
- Original Monster Card Forge content later

## Ruleset Separation

2014 and 2024 rules must be separated in the interface, data, packs, filters, and exports.

Rulesets may only be mixed when the user explicitly chooses to do so.

## Purchase / Pack Model

Future store filters should support:

- Individual cards
- Creature type packs
- CR range packs
- Environment / lair packs
- Curated adventure packs
- Homebrew/original packs

## MVP Standard

The MVP must make printable cards excellent before expanding into dice rollers, campaign builders, NPC generation, or magic items.
