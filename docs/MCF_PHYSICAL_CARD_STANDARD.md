# MCF Physical Card Standard

## MCF-001 Finished Card Size

Every standard Monster Card Forge card must finish at:

- 2.5 inches wide
- 3.5 inches tall

This is the standard trading card / poker card size and must fit common card sleeves and 9-pocket binder pages.

## MCF-002 Cut-and-Fold Standard Card

The unfolded cut-and-fold piece must be:

- 5.0 inches wide
- 3.5 inches tall

It is made from two 2.5 x 3.5 panels touching edge-to-edge.

## MCF-003 Fold Seam

The fold seam is a zero-width dotted score line where the right edge of the front panel touches the left edge of the back panel.

There is no spacer, hinge, gutter, or gap.

## MCF-004 Cutting Rule

Users cut only around the outside perimeter of the full 5 x 3.5 piece.

They do not cut the middle seam.

## MCF-005 Folding Rule

Users fold on the dotted seam, then laminate.

After folding, the card footprint must still be exactly 2.5 x 3.5.

## MCF-006 Boss Folio Rule

Boss Folio panels must also touch edge-to-edge.

Each panel is 2.5 x 3.5. Dotted fold seams appear only where adjacent panels touch.

No spacer columns are allowed.

## MCF-007 Product Promise

If a printable layout cannot preserve the finished 2.5 x 3.5 sleeve-compatible size, the layout is rejected and redesigned.
