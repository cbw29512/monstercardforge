# Market and SEO Research

## Research Summary

Monster Card Forge should be positioned as a premium printable and digital monster-card system, not just a PDF catalog.

The historical market proves the format has long-standing demand: TSR sold Monster Cards as 3 x 5 inch cards with monster art on one side and game statistics on the other, and older filing-card monster aids used the same basic physical reference concept. Our opportunity is to modernize that idea with web filtering, SRD ruleset separation, homebrew creation, print-ready layouts, and complex accordion cards.

## Competitive Observations

### What already exists

- Historic monster-card products prove physical monster references are a known tabletop accessory.
- Many digital tools focus on full stat blocks or encounter management, not premium printable cards.
- Many printable products are static PDFs, which limits filtering, homebrew, and card-format flexibility.

### Our Differentiators

1. Print-first design.
2. Website-first usability.
3. Free Goblin sample as the conversion hook.
4. Complete combat information, not stripped-down cards.
5. Smart layout engine: standard cards for simple monsters, accordion cards for complex monsters.
6. Homebrew builder with guided fields and live preview.
7. 2014 and 2024 ruleset separation.
8. Pack-based buying: single cards, type packs, CR packs, environment/lair packs, adventure packs later.

## Ecommerce SEO Requirements

Product pages should include structured product data when sales begin. Google Search Central recommends Product structured data for richer product search appearances, and merchant-listing data can include price, availability, shipping, return policy, and variants.

Required future implementation:

- Product JSON-LD per pack/card.
- Breadcrumb JSON-LD.
- FAQ JSON-LD on education pages.
- Unique title and meta description per pack.
- Human-readable product descriptions.
- Image alt text for card previews.
- Canonical URLs.
- XML sitemap.
- robots.txt that allows indexing of public pages.

### Higher-value products

- Accordion boss set.

## QC Requirements Before Selling

- Every card source is verified.
- Every export includes attribution.
- 2014 and 2024 cards are separated.
- All print files pass size checks.
- All pages have SEO metadata.
- All product pages have preview images.
- Checkout copy clearly states what the customer receives.

## Current Decision

We will keep building the card engine first. Store pages and SEO routes should be designed now, but actual sales wait until printable card quality and rules accuracy are production-ready.
