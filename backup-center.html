Recommended backup routine

Before a sessionDownload one full backup after prep is complete.
After a sessionArchive the session, update loot and NPCs, then export again.

Keep two copiesStore one locally and another in a private drive or external disk.

Privacy and limits

The full backup contains private DM information—including notes, NPC secrets, item curses, encounter tactics, and uploaded artwork—because its purpose is disaster recovery. Keep the downloaded file private. The backup covers recognized dmforge-* and cleric-box-* browser records on this GitHub Pages origin. Multiplayer connections themselves are not stored.
