What is saved on this device

Privacy and limits

Recovery points and campaign records stay inside this browser. A downloaded Safety Copy contains private DM information—including notes, NPC secrets, item curses, encounter tactics, and uploaded artwork—so keep that file private. Clearing all site data can remove local recovery points, which is why a Safety Copy remains useful before replacing a computer or resetting a browser.
