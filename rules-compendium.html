Accuracy boundary

Reference-complete does not automatically mean roll-automated.

Reference-complete

All 1,300 generated SRD records are searchable, edition-tagged, source-referenced, readable, and covered by generation checks.

Automation-complete

Weapons, core d20 cards, skills, saves, twenty-two spell families, and selected richer monster cards have structured executable behavior.

Never silently guessed

A readable reference remains a reference until its attacks, choices, scaling, saves, and edge cases have been structured and tested.

Free and local-first

No account, subscription, paid database, or analytics platform is required. Personal workspaces and homebrew records stay in this browser. DungeonCards personal tables and homebrew are not yet synchronized into Campaign Hub. Verified SRD monster selections may be deliberately sent to Encounter Forge on the same GitHub Pages origin; the transfer contains source IDs, names, edition, quantity, and campaign context, while Encounter Forge resolves authoritative combat values from the validated catalog. Homebrew transfer remains disabled until its complete versioned schema is tested.
